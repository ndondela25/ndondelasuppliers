About Us

Ndondela Suppliers Limited is a global general supply leader founded in 2019 with the goal to offer Products and services to public and private institutions across the global. NSL is focussing in supplying Food stuffs and beverages, Cosmetics and cleaning materials, Construction materials, Plumbing, Electrical, Furniture, Stationary, Motor Vehicles, and Automotive Spare parts. We understand your needs and we will deliver qaulity product and services to your door steps.

Mission Statement

To deliver quality products and services that meet customer need and requirements.
